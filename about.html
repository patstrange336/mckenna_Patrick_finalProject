What is Destination Creation?

Destination Creation is a project organization that helps people to create their own content creations online. This will allows people to market and make their own videos and web sites. We can help other people on how to create their own content and how to make it successful. These includes shopping sites, movie reviews, let's play videos, podcasts and more. In addition, this project will help the Internet run fairly and make it a place to run and work freely.


What can Destination Creation do to help?

This community is meant to welcome new content creators if they ever want to have a career on making videos on YouTube, movies and video games at Kickstarter, gaming channels on Twitch and so much more. Destination Creation can teach others on how to make a successful video or project. We also provide services if there is ever an obstacle that is keeping anyone away from completing their future project or career.


Why should I join this community?

With your support, you can help make a brighter future for the Internet. If you sign up now, you will receive updated news about the next Destination Creation conference or anything that happens on the Internet. You can also sign up as one of our speakers if you would like to have your voice heard. One voice can make a big difference.


How will Destination Creation change the future?

The Internet has been changing throughout the years both good and bad. We're trying remove some of the bad things that has been happening on the Internet i.e. demonetization, slow lanes or unfair YouTube claims. And instead replace them with positive ideas that both content creators and the system are treated equally. We want to welcome new creators and let them spread their creativity. The Internet is not going anywhere and neither will anyone else.
