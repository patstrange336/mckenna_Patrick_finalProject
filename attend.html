How to attend Destination Creation's conference?

You must have your ticket ready at all times. It will give you reserve parking and seating. You can wear any attire you like at the conference as along as it is appropriate. This also applies to speakers. If you signed up for Destination Creation, you will receive an email on when and where the next conference will take place.


How to buy tickets?

You must press the purchase button at the bottom the page and fill out a form. You must have a registered account to receive your ticket. If you already have an account, your tickets will arrive by mail in no more than three days prior to the event. If you have any problems when buying your tickets, please contact us.
